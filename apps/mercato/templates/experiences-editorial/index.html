"We designed this experience for people who have achieved everything on paper — but still feel like something is missing."

There's a particular kind of restlessness that comes with success. You've built the career, checked the boxes, filled the calendar. And yet, in the quiet moments, there's a pull toward something you can't quite name. Something slower. Deeper. More real.

This experience was born from that feeling. We bring together a small group of thoughtful people in an extraordinary setting, strip away the noise of daily life, and create the conditions for genuine presence. No agenda-driven networking. No performative wellness. Just space, craft, conversation, and the rare luxury of unhurried time.

Every element — from the hands-on workshops to the meals we share — is designed with the same intention: to help you reconnect with the parts of yourself that get lost in the rush.

The Itinerary

How Three Days Unfold

Day One

Arrive & Open

Arrive at the villa by late morning. After settling in, join the group for a welcome lunch on the terrace. The afternoon is spent on an introductory workshop and a slow walk through the olive groves. Evening brings the first of our long-table dinners under the stars.

Check-in from 11:00 AM
Day Two

Create & Explore

The heart of the experience. Morning begins with a guided practice, followed by the main workshop sessions. After lunch, venture out to local artisan studios and vineyards. Return for an evening of conversation, wine, and a cooking demonstration by our chef.

Full day, 8:00 AM – 10:00 PM
Day Three

Reflect & Depart

A quieter morning of reflection, journaling, and a closing circle. Share what you've discovered, exchange details with new friends, and enjoy a final breakfast together before departure. Take the afternoon to explore the nearby village on your own.

Check-out by 2:00 PM
