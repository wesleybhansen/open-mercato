What to Expect

Every detail, thoughtfully considered

Guided Sessions

Morning meditation, breathwork, movement, and evening reflection — all led by experienced practitioners.

Farm-Fresh Meals

Nature Immersion

The Journey

Three days of transformation

Photo
Day One — Arrival
Settling Into Stillness

Arrive at your own pace. Enjoy a welcome tea ceremony, settle into your accommodation, and join an opening circle as the sun sets. Tonight is about releasing what you've carried here.

Photo
Day Two — Immersion
Going Deeper

Morning breathwork and movement at sunrise. A farm-to-table brunch followed by a guided nature walk. Afternoon workshops in journaling, sound healing, or creative expression. Evening bonfire and stargazing.

Photo
Day Three — Integration
Carrying It Forward

A gentle morning practice followed by a closing circle. Share intentions, exchange gratitude, and leave with a personal integration guide to carry the stillness back into your daily life.
