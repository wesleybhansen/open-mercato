Our Story

It started with a simple question

What if the everyday objects we reach for were made with the same intention as the special ones? What if quality wasn't a luxury, but a standard?

That question led me to leave my career and spend two years learning traditional techniques from master artisans. I traveled, studied, failed, and eventually created something I was truly proud of.

Today, every piece we make carries that same curiosity and care. We're a small team, we work slowly, and we believe that's exactly what makes the difference.
