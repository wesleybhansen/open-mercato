Materials

Honest ingredients, nothing more.

Organic Cotton

GOTS-certified, grown without synthetic pesticides. Sourced from family farms in Portugal.

Plant-Based Dyes

Colors derived from indigo, madder root, and walnut hulls. Gentle on skin, gentle on the earth.

Brass Hardware

Solid brass, not plated. Develops a warm patina over time that makes each piece uniquely yours.

Recycled Packaging

100% post-consumer recycled kraft paper. Compostable, minimal, and entirely plastic-free.

Natural Beeswax

Applied as a finish coat for protection. Ethically harvested from small-scale apiaries.

Our Story

Born from a simple question.

What if the objects we use every day were made with the same care as the moments they're part of? We started Æther in a small workshop with that question and a belief that thoughtful design shouldn't be reserved for special occasions.

Today, every product we make still begins the same way — with a conversation about what matters. No trends, no shortcuts. Just honest materials shaped by skilled hands into objects that quietly improve your daily life.

Elena Aström
Founder & Maker

Our Commitments

Better by nature.

Carbon Neutral Shipping

Every order ships carbon neutral. We offset 110% of our logistics emissions through verified reforestation projects.

Recyclable Packaging

Zero plastic, always. Our packaging is 100% post-consumer recycled and fully compostable. Designed to disappear.

1% for the Planet

We donate 1% of annual revenue to environmental nonprofits. Building a business that gives back isn't optional — it's foundational.

Questions

Things people ask.

Domestic orders typically ship within 2-3 business days and arrive in 5-7 days. International orders take 7-14 business days depending on the destination. All orders include tracking.

We offer full refunds or exchanges within 30 days of delivery, provided items are in their original condition. We'll send you a prepaid return label — no hassle, no questions.

All products are made in our workshop in Portland, Oregon, with materials sourced primarily from Portugal, Japan, and the Pacific Northwest. We visit every supplier in person.

Each product ships with a detailed care card. Generally, our items improve with age and regular use. Spot clean cotton items, avoid harsh chemicals on ceramics, and let leather develop its natural patina.
