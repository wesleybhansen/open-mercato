Frequently asked questions

Each experience listing clearly states what's included. Typically this covers the activity itself, any materials or equipment needed, and sometimes food and drinks. Multi-day experiences often include accommodations. Check the specific listing for full details.
Most experiences offer free cancellation up to 48 hours before the start date. Some experiences with limited spots may have different policies. The cancellation policy is always displayed before you book.
Click "Gift an Experience" and choose the experience you'd like to send. You can add a personal message and choose the delivery date. The recipient receives a digital gift card they can redeem for any available date.
Each listing includes a difficulty level and physical requirements. We offer everything from gentle wellness retreats to challenging adventure activities. Use our filters to find experiences that match your comfort level.
Many hosts offer private group bookings for parties, team events, or celebrations. Look for the "Private Group" option on the experience page, or contact the host directly to arrange a custom date and group size.
Click "Become a Host" and fill out our application. We review all applications to ensure quality. Once approved, you can create your experience listing, set your dates and pricing, and start hosting within days.
